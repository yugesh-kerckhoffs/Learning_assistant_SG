📊 Data Collection & Monitoring

Important Notice for Parents and Guardians:

  • Chat History: We collect and store all chat conversations between the child and the AI assistant.
  • Usage Data: We track learning activities, game progress, and interaction patterns.
  • Generated Content: Images and videos created by the child are stored for review purposes.
  • Purpose: This data is collected to:
    • Monitor child safety and appropriate content usage

    • Report concerning behavior to designated supervisors

  • Access: Parents, guardians, and authorized educational staff may access this data at any time.
  • Alerts: Inappropriate requests or concerning patterns will trigger immediate notifications to supervisors.
